# Baby Hippo Coin (BHC) — v0.1

## Purpose

BHC is a testnet learning and participation reward for the Baby Hippo community.

Its purpose in v0.1 is to encourage education, responsible product testing, constructive feedback, and steady participation. It is not a shortcut to wealth and should never distract from the project's first priorities: risk awareness, financial literacy, and community growth.

## Community Context

Baby Hippo is built for ordinary people who work hard every day, including drivers, logistics and labor workers, music teachers, grassroots arts educators, rural communities, and people trying to improve their lives.

BHC should recognize effort and learning without creating pressure to speculate. Rewards should make a newcomer feel welcomed and proud of progress—not afraid of missing out.

## v0.1 Uses

- Completing Hippo Academy lessons and knowledge checks.
- Learning how to read a Lobster Watch alert.
- Testing DCA and lending simulations.
- Providing useful product feedback.
- Participating constructively in approved community activities.
- Claiming limited faucet tokens for testnet testing.

## v0.1 Communication Rules

Do not describe BHC as:

- A guaranteed investment opportunity
- A path to overnight wealth
- A token certain to increase in price
- A reward for recruiting people into speculation
- Proof that future mainnet benefits are guaranteed

Describe BHC clearly as a **testnet learning and participation reward with no promised monetary value**.

## Recommended Constraints

- Testnet deployment only.
- Fixed and capped test supply.
- No presale, fundraising, price promises, or implied investment return.
- No governance authority over user funds.
- No transfer tax, hidden minting, or opaque administrative power.
- Verified source code and a publicly documented contract address.
- Rate-limited distribution and anti-abuse monitoring.
- Rewards tied primarily to learning and constructive participation, not transaction volume.

## Decisions Required Before Deployment

- Target testnet.
- Total test supply and decimal precision.
- Whether BHC should be transferable during v0.1.
- Reward amounts for lessons, testing, and feedback.
- Anti-abuse limits and eligibility rules.
- Admin ownership model and emergency controls.
- Conditions for pausing, retiring, or migrating the test token.

## Deferred Token Topics

Mainnet issuance, token sales, allocation, vesting, governance, staking, liquidity, treasury policy, and regulatory classification are outside v0.1.

These topics require separate community, economic, security, and legal review. A successful testnet learning program does not automatically justify a mainnet token.

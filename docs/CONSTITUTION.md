# Baby Hippo Constitution

> **From Worker To On-Chain Boss**  
> **從上班族，成為鏈上老闆**

## Preamble

Baby Hippo was created for ordinary people who work hard, carry real responsibilities, and still hope to build a better future.

This Constitution defines the permanent values of the Baby Hippo community. Products may change. Technology may change. Markets may rise and fall. But these principles should remain.

They belong to every driver, worker, teacher, artist, small business owner, rural family, learner, builder, and community member who walks this road with us.

No token price, market trend, partnership, or short-term opportunity should be allowed to replace these values.

## Article 1 — Respect Honest Work

We respect people who earn through honest work.

Truck drivers, logistics workers, labor workers, teachers, artists, small business owners, and ordinary workers deserve financial education and opportunities too.

No honest occupation is too ordinary to be respected. No person's voice should matter less because they come from a rural town, have limited capital, or are new to technology.

Baby Hippo will remember the dignity behind every working day.

## Article 2 — Long-Term Growth

We believe sustainable growth is more important than short-term speculation.

Real growth may be slow. It comes through learning, saving, discipline, patience, better habits, and decisions that can survive difficult times.

We will not measure every person's progress by speed, portfolio size, or short-term performance. Building knowledge and avoiding a preventable mistake are also meaningful forms of growth.

## Article 3 — No Scams

Baby Hippo rejects fraud, deception, exploitation, pump-and-dump behavior, fake promises, and dishonest practices.

We will not build community growth on manipulation, hidden conflicts, false urgency, or the financial harm of others.

We will not glorify hacking, exploitation, or taking advantage of people who understand less. If something cannot be explained honestly, it should not be promoted by Baby Hippo.

## Article 4 — Education Before Speculation

Learning comes before investing.  
Understanding comes before leverage.

People should know what a wallet is, what can go wrong, what they are signing, and what they may lose before considering an action.

Baby Hippo will encourage questions. Beginners should never be shamed for not knowing a technical term. Clear education is part of safety.

## Article 5 — Risk Management First

Protecting capital is more important than chasing returns.

We believe people should understand concentration, scams, liquidation, smart-contract risk, data limitations, and their own financial boundaries.

No return is guaranteed. Higher advertised rewards may carry higher or hidden risks. Baby Hippo should show the risk before presenting the possible opportunity.

## Article 6 — Financial Tools For Everyone

Financial education, risk management, and modern tools should not be reserved only for whales and institutions.

Ordinary people deserve tools written in language they can understand, designed around the time and resources they actually have.

Baby Hippo will work to make on-chain knowledge more approachable without pretending that complicated systems are risk-free.

## Article 7 — Community Over Token

People are more important than the token.  
The community is more important than price action.

BHC exists to support testnet learning and participation in v0.1. The community does not exist to serve BHC.

No member should be pressured to buy, hold, promote, or reveal financial information to belong. Trust, education, helpful participation, and care for one another are more valuable than transaction volume or token attention.

If the token and the well-being of the community ever point in different directions, Baby Hippo must choose the community.

## Article 8 — Build A Better Future

The goal of Baby Hippo is not to create overnight millionaires.

The goal is to help ordinary people improve life step by step through education, discipline, community, and technology.

A better future may mean understanding a first wallet, recognizing a scam, building a steady saving habit, managing risk more carefully, supporting a family, teaching another beginner, or creating new possibilities in a small town.

We do not define success for every person. We build tools and learning paths that help people make more informed choices for themselves.

## How This Constitution Guides Us

This Constitution applies to Baby Hippo's products, education, community events, partnerships, stories, rewards, and public communication.

Before making an important decision, we should ask:

- Does this respect honest work and ordinary people?
- Does this teach clearly before encouraging action?
- Are the risks visible and understandable?
- Does this depend on hype, pressure, deception, or unrealistic promises?
- Does this strengthen the community, or only attract attention to a token?
- Would we be comfortable explaining this decision to a driver, worker, teacher, or first-time learner face to face?

If a proposal conflicts with these principles, short-term growth is not a reason to accept it.

## Founder Statement

> We do not promise easy money.
>

>
> **From Worker To On-Chain Boss.**

# Baby Hippo Product Specification

## Positioning

**為每一位努力生活的人打造的鏈上成長社群。**  
**An on-chain growth community built for everyone working hard to improve life.**

Baby Hippo is a community-led learning and risk-management product. Financial tools are part of the experience, but they serve a larger mission: helping ordinary people build knowledge, confidence, safer habits, and a long-term digital asset moat.

> **From Worker To On-Chain Boss**  
> **從上班族，成為鏈上老闆**

Becoming an on-chain boss does not mean getting rich overnight. It means gradually learning how wallets, risk, DCA, lending, and digital ownership work—and becoming more capable of making independent decisions.

## Founder-Led Product Context

The founder comes from a rural town in Miaoli, Taiwan. His experience spans logistics and freight transportation as well as violin teaching.

The product should remain grounded in the lives of:

- Transportation and freight drivers
- Logistics and labor workers
- Violin and piano teachers
- Grassroots arts education workers
- People from rural towns and communities
- Ordinary people working to improve their lives

Product language must be practical, respectful, and free of insider arrogance. Baby Hippo should never assume that users have abundant free time, large amounts of capital, or prior knowledge of Web3.

## Product Values

### Honest Growth

We reject scams, fake promises, hacker-style exploitation, and pump-and-dump culture. Product growth should come from trust, usefulness, and community contribution.

### Long-Term Compounding

We teach steady habits and patience rather than overnight gambling. Compounding refers to the long-term accumulation of knowledge, discipline, community trust, and potentially assets—not a guaranteed financial outcome.

### Education Before Speculation

People should understand what they are looking at before considering any action. Every major concept should have a plain-language explanation.

### Risk Management Before Leverage

The product should make downside, liquidation, concentration, and data limitations visible. Leverage execution is not part of v0.1.

### Built for Ordinary People

The interface must work for beginners and people with limited time or capital—not only experienced traders or large holders.

### Community Support

Baby Hippo should create a welcoming path for people from grassroots and rural backgrounds to learn together without shame or intimidation.

## v0.1 Core Journey

1. **Connect:** The user connects a supported wallet.
2. **Understand:** Baby Hippo summarizes holdings in plain language.
3. **Protect:** Lobster Watch identifies important risks first.
4. **Learn:** Hippo Academy explains the concepts behind those risks.
5. **Plan:** Hippo Bank helps the user create a simulated DCA plan and understand lending basics.
6. **Progress:** The user earns testnet BHC for learning and responsible participation.
7. **Return:** The community and product help the user continue building knowledge and steady habits.

## Product Priority and Boundaries

### Priority 1 — Lobster Watch

Lobster Watch is the v0.1 flagship.

It monitors portfolio and market inputs, calculates deterministic risk signals, and produces evidence-based alerts. AI translates structured findings into understandable language but cannot invent scores, promise outcomes, or execute transactions.

Initial signals include:

- Portfolio concentration
- Stablecoin depeg exposure
- Protocol concentration
- Lending health factor
- Unrecognized token or protocol exposure
- Unusually high or rapidly changing rates
- Stale price or market data

### Priority 2 — Hippo Bank

Hippo Bank is a planning and financial-literacy tool. In v0.1 it includes:

- DCA planning and simulation
- Read-only lending market comparison
- Plain-language explanations of supply, borrowing, APY, collateral, and liquidation
- A simple health-factor simulator

It does not custody assets, automatically rebalance positions, or promise that a strategy will be profitable.

### Priority 3 — Hippo Academy

Hippo Academy is the learning home of the community.

Initial learning paths should cover:

- Wallet and seed phrase safety
- Understanding tokens and networks
- DCA fundamentals
- DeFi lending basics
- APY and compounding
- Collateral, health factor, and liquidation
- Common scams and pump-and-dump warning signs
- How to read a Lobster Watch alert

Lessons should be short enough for a driver, worker, or teacher to complete during a break. Progress should emphasize understanding rather than trading activity.

### Priority 4 — Baby Hippo Coin

In v0.1, BHC is a testnet learning and participation reward. It can recognize completed lessons, responsible testing, feedback, and helpful community participation.

BHC is not marketed as an investment, has no promised monetary value, and does not represent guaranteed future benefits.

### Priority 5 — Hippo Farm

Hippo Farm is a future exploration feature and is not part of the v0.1 core product.

If explored later, it should present carefully reviewed yield opportunities with transparent sources, risks, lockups, and exit conditions. It must never become a feed that rewards the highest advertised yield without context.

## Key v0.1 Screens

- Community welcome and founder message
- Safety orientation
- Wallet connection
- Portfolio overview
- Lobster Watch risk dashboard
- Alert detail with evidence and plain-language explanation
- Hippo Bank DCA planner
- Lending education and read-only comparison
- Hippo Academy lessons and progress
- Learning quests and testnet BHC rewards
- Settings, data sources, feedback, and disclaimers

## Explicit v0.1 Exclusions

- Hippo Farm opportunity listings
- Fiat deposits or withdrawals
- Custody of user assets
- Mainnet trading
- Automated investment execution
- Cross-chain bridging
- Leveraged strategy execution
- BHC sales, price promises, or investment marketing
- Anonymous personalization using sensitive financial data
- Unreviewed AI-generated transaction data

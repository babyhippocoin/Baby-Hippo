# 1. Company Overview

## What Baby Hippo Is

Baby Hippo Coin (BHC) is an **education-first Web3 learning platform** designed specifically for ordinary working people. Founded by someone with lived experience in logistics, freight work, and violin teaching in rural Taiwan, Baby Hippo bridges the gap between traditional finance and decentralized systems through accessible education and habit-building tools.

## Mission Statement

**To help ordinary working people build long-term financial knowledge and on-chain habits through education, discipline, and responsible technology.**

## Core Philosophy

- **Education before speculation** - Users learn before they invest
- **Trust before growth** - Sustainable relationships over rapid scaling
- **Discipline before profit** - Consistent habits over perfect timing
- **Community before token** - People matter more than price action

## What Makes Us Different

**Not an exchange, trading platform, or investment service.** Baby Hippo is an educational ecosystem that teaches financial literacy, wallet security, DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) discipline, and DeFi concepts without holding user funds or providing financial advice.

**Target Audience:** Truck drivers, teachers, logistics workers, farmers, small business owners, students, and families - especially those in Taiwan and rural communities often overlooked by financial technology.

## Business Model

Baby Hippo operates through:
- Educational partnerships with exchanges (BitoPro, Binance, OKX)
- Community-driven word-of-mouth growth
- Future premium educational content and certification programs
- Maintaining strict boundaries against speculation-driven revenue

## Current Status

**Beta Phase** with live features including:
- Comprehensive Web3 education curriculum
- Lobster Watch discipline tracking system
- BHC Points educational achievement system
- Bilingual (Traditional Chinese/English) interface
- Taiwan fiat on-ramp guidance through BitoPro

**Next Phase:** Enhanced verification systems, mobile optimization, and community expansion across Southeast Asia.

# 8. Glossary

## Core Baby Hippo Terms

### BHC (Baby Hippo Coin)
**Definition:** Educational achievement points that reward learning progress and disciplined financial habits. NOT a cryptocurrency, investment, or tradeable token.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Local storage only, no blockchain transactions
- Non-transferable and non-monetary value
- Earned through education completion and habit consistency
- Used for progress tracking and motivation only

**Usage:** "Users earn BHC Points for completing Bitcoin education modules and maintaining DCA discipline."

### Lobster Watch
**Definition:** Discipline companion tool that helps users maintain long-term investment habits through reminders, tracking, and education - never through trading signals or financial advice.

**Core Functions:**
- DCA reminder scheduling with gentle, non-pressuring notifications
- Habit consistency tracking and progress visualization
- Risk education and portfolio monitoring (future)
- Educational milestone celebration and motivation

**Philosophy:** Focuses on building sustainable habits rather than predicting market movements or optimizing trading performance.

### DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging)
**Definition:** Investment strategy of buying fixed amounts of assets at regular intervals, regardless of price, to reduce timing risk and emotional decision-making.

**Baby Hippo Approach:**
- Educational focus on why DCA works for busy working people
- Emphasis on consistency over perfect timing
- Integration with user work schedules and financial constraints
- No automated execution - users maintain full control

**Example:** "A truck driver invests $50 in Bitcoin every two weeks, regardless of whether the price is $30,000 or $70,000."

### DeFi (Decentralized Finance)
**Definition:** Financial services built on blockchain networks that operate without traditional intermediaries like banks, using smart contracts for automation.

**Baby Hippo Education:**
- Progressive introduction after Bitcoin and Ethereum mastery
- Risk-first education covering smart contract vulnerabilities
- Focus on conservative strategies and capital preservation
- Emphasis on understanding before participation

**Key Risks Taught:** Smart contract bugs, protocol governance changes, liquidation mechanisms, and market volatility amplification.

## Technical and Security Terms

### Wallet
**Definition:** Software or hardware that stores cryptocurrency private keys and enables interaction with blockchain networks.

**Types Covered:**
- **Custodial:** Exchange-held wallets where users don't control private keys
- **Non-custodial:** Self-custody wallets where users control their own keys
- **Hardware:** Physical devices for offline private key storage
- **Software:** Applications for convenient but less secure key management

**Baby Hippo Security Education:** Private key protection, seed phrase backup, and recovery procedures.

### APR (Annual Percentage Rate)
**Definition:** Yearly rate earned on cryptocurrency through staking, lending, or liquidity provision, expressed as a percentage.

**Baby Hippo Context:**
- Explanation that high APR often indicates higher risk
- Comparison with traditional savings and investment returns
- Education about sustainability and protocol risk factors
- Warning about "too good to be true" yield opportunities

### Aave
**Definition:** Decentralized lending protocol that allows users to lend and borrow cryptocurrencies using smart contracts.

**Baby Hippo Integration:**
- Advanced education module unlocked after foundational learning
- Focus on Health Factor understanding and liquidation risk
- Conservative borrowing strategies and risk management
- Read-only monitoring integration in Lobster Watch

### Read-Only
**Definition:** Data access that allows viewing information without the ability to make changes or execute transactions.

**Baby Hippo Implementation:**
- Portfolio monitoring without custody or control of funds
- Wallet address observation without private key access
- Educational verification without trading permissions
- Habit validation without financial transaction capability

**Privacy Protection:** Users share public addresses voluntarily; no access to private keys or transaction signing required.

# 9. Frequently Asked Questions

## About Baby Hippo's Mission and Purpose

### What exactly is Baby Hippo?
Baby Hippo is an educational platform that teaches working people about cryptocurrency and decentralized finance (DeFi) safely and responsibly. We're not an exchange, investment service, or trading platform. We provide education, habit-building tools, and community support to help ordinary people understand and use modern financial technology.

### Why was Baby Hippo created?
Baby Hippo was founded by someone with lived experience as a logistics worker and violin teacher in rural Taiwan. Most crypto education assumes technical knowledge, risk tolerance, and disposable income that working people don't have. Baby Hippo fills this gap with education designed for truck drivers, teachers, small business owners, and families.

### How is Baby Hippo different from other crypto platforms?
We prioritize education over speculation, long-term habits over short-term trading, and user safety over engagement metrics. We don't promise quick profits, encourage day trading, or use high-pressure tactics. Every feature teaches before asking users to act, and we celebrate consistency over portfolio performance.

### Is Baby Hippo safe for beginners?
Yes. Baby Hippo is specifically designed for people with zero cryptocurrency experience. We start with basic concepts, use plain language explanations, provide comprehensive scam prevention education, and never request private keys or seed phrases. Users learn at their own pace with no financial pressure.

## About BHC Points and Rewards

### Are BHC Points a cryptocurrency I can trade?
No. BHC Points are educational achievement markers stored locally on your device. They have no monetary value, cannot be traded or transferred, and are not blockchain tokens. They exist purely to track your learning progress and celebrate your educational milestones.

### How do I earn BHC Points?
You earn points by completing educational modules, maintaining consistent DCA habits, contributing to community learning, and demonstrating understanding of financial concepts. Points reward education and discipline, never trading profits or portfolio performance.

### What can I do with my BHC Points?
BHC Points unlock advanced educational content, show your progress through different competency levels (Explorer to Chain Boss), and can be shared with the community to inspire others. They're personal achievement markers, not financial assets.

### Will BHC Points ever become tradeable tokens?
No. BHC Points will always remain educational achievements without monetary value. Converting them to tradeable tokens would undermine our educational mission and create speculation incentives that harm our users' learning focus.

## About Lobster Watch and Tools

### What is Lobster Watch and how does it work?
Lobster Watch is a habit-building companion that helps you maintain consistent investment discipline. It sends gentle reminders for your DCA schedule, tracks your consistency, and provides educational alerts about portfolio risks. It never gives trading signals or investment advice.

### Does Lobster Watch automatically buy cryptocurrency for me?
No. Lobster Watch reminds you about your planned investment schedule and provides educational information, but you maintain complete control over all financial decisions and transactions. We never execute trades or access your money.

### Can Lobster Watch predict market movements?
No. Lobster Watch focuses on helping you maintain disciplined habits regardless of market conditions. We don't predict prices or claim to know market direction. Instead, we teach you why consistent habits matter more than perfect timing.

### Is my financial information private when using Lobster Watch?
Yes. Lobster Watch can monitor public wallet addresses if you choose to share them, but we never request private keys, passwords, or account access. Most tracking happens locally on your device, and we follow strict privacy protection principles.

## About Education and Learning

### Do I need technical knowledge to use Baby Hippo?
No. Baby Hippo is designed for complete beginners. We explain everything in plain language, use familiar analogies, and progress from simple concepts to more complex topics only when you're ready. Technical background helps but isn't required.

### How long does it take to complete Baby Hippo's education?
Learning happens at your own pace. Basic Bitcoin and wallet security education can be completed in a few hours, but developing good investment habits and understanding advanced DeFi concepts takes months. We prioritize understanding over speed.

### Can I access Baby Hippo content offline?
Currently, most content requires internet access, but we're developing offline capabilities for core educational modules. This is especially important for users in rural areas or with limited connectivity during work hours.

### Does Baby Hippo provide financial advice?
No. We provide educational information and tools to help you make your own informed decisions. We never recommend specific investments, guarantee returns, or replace professional financial advisors. Education empowers choice; it doesn't make choices for you.

## About Community and Support

### How do I connect with other Baby Hippo users?
Our community features allow you to share learning progress, ask questions, and support others' educational journeys. We're developing regional chapters for local meetups and mentorship programs for peer learning support.

### Can I teach others what I learn through Baby Hippo?
Yes! We encourage users to share knowledge with family and community members. Teaching others reinforces your own learning and helps build financially literate communities. Just remember to focus on education, not investment advice.

### What if I have questions while learning?
You can access our help resources, community forums, and future AI tutors for 24/7 learning support. We're also developing mentorship matching to connect beginners with experienced community members who can provide guidance.

### How does Baby Hippo prevent scams within the community?
We have strict community guidelines prohibiting investment advice, get-rich-quick schemes, and promotional content. Our moderation systems and community reporting help identify and remove harmful content quickly.

## About Technology and Security

### What technology does Baby Hippo use?
We use modern web technology (Next.js, React, TypeScript) hosted on Cloudflare for security and performance. Our AI agents use advanced language models for educational support and content creation, always under human oversight.

### How does Baby Hippo protect my data?
We collect minimal data necessary for educational services, use strong encryption, and give you control over your information. Most progress tracking happens locally on your device. We never sell user data or use it for marketing without consent.

### What happens if Baby Hippo's website goes down?
We maintain backup systems and recovery procedures. Your locally stored progress (like BHC Points) remains on your device. Critical educational content will be accessible through multiple channels to ensure continuity of learning.

### Do you plan to develop mobile apps?
Yes. We're prioritizing mobile-friendly web interfaces first, then developing dedicated apps with offline learning capability, push notifications for educational reminders, and enhanced accessibility features.

## About Business Model and Sustainability

### How does Baby Hippo make money?
We operate through educational partnerships with established exchanges (with clear disclosure), future premium educational content, and certification programs. We don't take trading fees, sell user data, or profit from user losses.

### Is Baby Hippo a non-profit organization?
While we operate with educational mission focus, we're structured as a sustainable business that can fund long-term development and community support. Profits are reinvested in educational content, platform improvement, and community growth.

### Will Baby Hippo always be free to use?
Core educational content and basic features will always remain free. We may introduce premium features like advanced analytics, certification programs, or enhanced community tools, but fundamental financial education will remain accessible to everyone.

### What regions does Baby Hippo serve?
We currently focus on Taiwan with Traditional Chinese and English content. Expansion plans include Southeast Asia (Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia) with localized content and partnerships, then broader global expansion based on community needs.

# Baby Hippo

> **From Worker To On-Chain Boss**

## An On-Chain Growth Community for Ordinary People

**為每一位努力生活的人打造的鏈上成長社群。**  
**An on-chain growth community built for everyone working hard to improve life.**

Baby Hippo is not only a Web3 finance tool. It is a learning, risk-management, and mutual-support community for ordinary people who want to understand digital assets and improve their lives step by step.

We begin with people who are often overlooked by financial technology: transportation and freight drivers, labor workers, violin and piano teachers, grassroots arts educators, people from rural communities, and everyone building a better future through honest work.

## Founder Story

Baby Hippo comes from real life.

The founder grew up in a rural town in Miaoli, Taiwan. He has worked in logistics and freight transportation and also teaches violin. Through these experiences, he understands the daily reality of drivers, labor workers, music teachers, piano and violin teachers, and grassroots arts education workers.

These are people who work long hours, carry real responsibilities, and rarely have time to decode complicated financial systems. Baby Hippo is being built with them—not as an abstract market segment, but as the community the founder knows and belongs to.

## Version 0.1 Goal

Version 0.1 will deliver a safe, testnet-first learning experience where a user can:

1. Connect a wallet and understand basic holdings.
2. Receive clear risk signals from Lobster Watch.
3. Learn why each risk signal matters.
4. Create and simulate a DCA plan through Hippo Bank.
5. Learn essential wallet, DeFi, and risk concepts in Hippo Academy.
6. Earn testnet BHC for learning and responsible participation.

Version 0.1 is an educational decision-support product. It does not custody user funds, promise returns, encourage leverage, or allow AI to execute autonomous transactions.

## v0.1 Product Principles

- **Protect before promoting:** Explain risk before discussing possible opportunity.
- **Teach before asking users to act:** Beginners deserve context, not pressure.
- **Explain every score:** Every warning should show its reason and evidence.
- **Testnet first:** Learn safely before introducing real-value activity.
- **Non-custodial:** Users remain in control of their wallets.
- **AI explains; rules decide:** Deterministic systems calculate risk and AI translates it into plain language.
- **Progress over hype:** Reward learning, consistency, and responsible participation.

## Success Metrics

- 100 testnet users connect a wallet.
- 70% view and understand at least one Lobster Watch finding.
- 60% complete a foundational Hippo Academy lesson.
- 50% create at least one simulated DCA plan.
- 30% return within seven days.
- 80% of surveyed users can explain why their risk score changed.
- Zero critical security issues in the v0.1 testnet release.
